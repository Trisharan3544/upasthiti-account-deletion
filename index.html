How Account Deletion Works

This page allows you to permanently delete your Upasthiti account and all associated data. The deletion process is immediate and irreversible.

  • Authentication Required: You must log in with your Upasthiti account credentials to proceed with deletion.
  • Re-Authentication: For security, you will be required to re-enter your password before deletion.
  • Immediate Deletion: Once confirmed, your account and all data will be permanently deleted.
  • No Recovery: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email address, name, phone number, and profile information
  • Classes and Departments: All classes, departments, and organizational data you created
  • Student Information: All student records including names, roll numbers, contact details, and photos
  • Attendance Records: All attendance data, history, and reports
  • Uploaded Files: All student photos and other files stored in your account
  • Settings and Preferences: All app settings and user preferences

All personal data associated with your Upasthiti account is permanently deleted.

Data Retention Policy

No data is retained after account deletion.

  • Deletion is immediate and irreversible
  • All data is permanently removed from our systems
  • No backups or copies of your data are retained
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion
